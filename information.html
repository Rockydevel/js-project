The Railway Enquiries Instructions on Call are as follows:



For IRCTC PNR status: Press 1 and enter your assigned ten-digit PNR number carefully.

For train arrival, departure, cancellation and diversion status: Press 2 and proceed with the instructions.

For seat availability: Press 3 and proceed with the instructions.


For fare enquiry: Press 4 and proceed with the instructions.

For booking train tickets through credit and debit cards: Press 5 and follow the instructions

For cancellation of reserved Indian railway train tickets: Press 6 and follow the instructions.

For wake-up call or destination alert: Press 7 and follow the instructions.


For booking wheelchairs at a Railway station: Press 8 and proceed with the instructions to book wheelchairs at the railway station.

For booking meals in train: Press 9 and follow the instructions.
