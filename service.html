IRCTC PNR Status SMS Enquiry


1.Format: PNR <10 Digit PNR Number>
Example: PNR 2356969690

2.SMS Enquiry for Arrival or Departure of train
Format: AD .
Example: AD 12352 0612

3.SMS Enquiry for Seat Availability of General Quota
Format: SEAT
Example: SEAT 12008 080720 080 044 CC

5.SMS Enquiry for Indian Railway Time Table
Format: TIME
Example: TIME 12351

6.Train Fare Enquiry through SMS
Format: FARE
Example: FARE 12008 080720 080 044 2A G

7.For Setting Destination Alert
Format: ALERT <10 Digit PNR Number>
Example: ALERT 2356969690
